Hello, my name is Valeriy Lindstrem, and I am a pastry chef working in Moscow. This is my small project for baking desserts by pre-order. I am delighted to invite you to join this delicious experience.

For each dessert, I use high-quality ingredients, from flour to berries and flowers. I take into account the seasonality of fruits to maintain the quality of taste. You can also pre-order a dessert that is not listed among the offered ones. I will gladly consider and, if possible, fulfill your wishes.

You can also order catering for your celebration or work event. Additionally, I am happy to organize a personal or company workshop for you, where we will together prepare dessert(s) of your choice.
